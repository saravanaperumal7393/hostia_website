About Us

At HOSTIA, we are the driving force behind the growth and development of small and tiny industries in Hosur. Our association is committed to fostering a thriving ecosystem that empowers entrepreneurs, drives innovation, and champions the interests of our members.

We believe in the potential of small and tiny industries as pivotal contributors to Hosur's economic landscape. HOSTIA is dedicated to providing comprehensive support, resources, and a collaborative platform that enables these enterprises to flourish.
